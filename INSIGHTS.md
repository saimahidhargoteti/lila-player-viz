Insights from Player Behavior



\---



1\. High Traffic Zones Lead to Combat Hotspots



Observation



Heatmap shows strong clustering of player movement in specific central areas.



Evidence



\* Dense overlapping paths in certain map regions

\* High concentration of event markers (kills/deaths)



Impact



\* Increased combat frequency

\* Early player eliminations



Actionable Suggestions



\* Redistribute spawn points

\* Add alternative traversal routes

\* Balance loot across regions



Why Designers Should Care



Improves pacing and prevents overcrowding in early gameplay.



\---



2\. Bots Exhibit Predictable Movement Patterns



Observation



Bot paths are more linear and less dynamic compared to human players.



Evidence



\* Red paths (bots) show low variability

\* Less deviation and exploration



Impact



\* Easier for players to exploit bots

\* Reduced gameplay challenge



Actionable Suggestions



\* Introduce randomness in bot paths

\* Add adaptive AI behavior



Why Designers Should Care



Improves engagement and realism of gameplay.



\---



3\. Edge Areas Are Underutilized



Observation



Map edges show very low player activity.



Evidence



\* Sparse paths in boundary regions

\* Low heatmap intensity



Impact



\* Large portions of map remain unused

\* Reduced exploration



Actionable Suggestions



\* Place high-value loot at edges

\* Introduce side objectives or missions



Why Designers Should Care



Maximizes map usage and improves player exploration behavior.
